Women are the fastest growing population in U.S. Prison

The Women's Community Justice Association represents the women held at Rikers Island

93% of women at Rikers are survivors of domestic violence. Seventy percent of the women incarcerating are the primary caregivers for their families. Michelle Feldman, the policy director for WCJA, addressed the prison administration: “Please let more mothers, daughters and sisters spend the holidays where they belong.”
